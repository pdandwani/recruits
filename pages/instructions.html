INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Total time allotted to solve the paper is 30 mins.
  2. The total number of questions is 30.
  3. Questions are divided into 2 sections, Aptitude and General Knowledge. Each section has 15 questions.
  4. Any of the section can be attempted first, and the student can jump in between sections.
  5. Each question carries 3 marks with no negative marks.
  6. Some questions with ** beside them are considered a bonus question and carry 4 marks for a right answer and -1 for a wrong answer.
